## What We Do

NeuroTechX is a volunteer-led non-profit bridging the gap between academic neuroscience and the hands-on world of technology. We advance neurotechnology through three pillars:

- **Community** ‚Äî 30+ city chapters, 17,000+ local members, and a 9,500+ member Slack connecting researchers, engineers, students, and enthusiasts worldwide
- **Education** ‚Äî The [NeuroTech Primer](https://neurotechx.org/education), NeuroTechEDU tutorials, industry webinars from g.tec, Interaxon, Neuroelectrics, and more, plus the [FUS Forward podcast](https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/fus-forward/id1857021050)
- **Professional Development** ‚Äî A [jobs board](https://neurotechx.org/jobs), the [PURSUE](https://pursue.richmond.edu/) undergraduate research program, the [Neurotech Micro-credential Program](https://neurotechmicrocreds.com/) at Queen's University, and global hackathons via [Brainhack](https://brainhack.org/)
